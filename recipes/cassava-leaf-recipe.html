Ingredients

  • Fresh cassava leaf
  • Palm oil
  • Fish
  • Groundnut paste
  • Peper
  • Onion
  • Magie
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add water to the pot on fire
  2. Add the cassava leaf and allow to boil
  3. Add groundnut paste
  4. Add palm oil
  5. Add fish
  6. Add grind peper with onion, magie, and salt
